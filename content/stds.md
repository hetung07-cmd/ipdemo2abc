Tổng hợp các bộ tiêu chuẩn quan trọng trong ngành bán dẫn, dùng xuyên suốt technology transfer và vận hành fab.

## JEDEC (JEDEC Solid State Technology Association)

Chuẩn về reliability, qualification, test:

- **JEP001** — Foundry Process Qualification Guidelines
- **JEP012A** — Wafer-level reliability (HCI, TDDB, BTI, P²ID)
- **JEP119A** — Wafer-level EM (SWEAT)
- **JEP131A** — FMEA
- **JEP132A** — Process Characterization
- **JESD22** series — Reliability test methods (HTOL, HAST, TC, ESD)
- **JESD46** — Customer notification (PCN)
- **JESD47** — Stress-Test-Driven Qualification
- **JESD85** — FIT rate calculation

## AEC (Automotive Electronics Council)

Chuẩn cho linh kiện automotive:

- **AEC-Q100** — IC qualification
- **AEC-Q101** — Discrete qualification
- **AEC-Q006** — Cu wire bonding

## SEMI (Semiconductor Equipment and Materials International)

Chuẩn về thiết bị, vật liệu, automation:

- **SEMI E10** — Equipment reliability/availability metrics
- **SEMI E30 (GEM)** — Generic Equipment Model
- **SEMI E37 (HSMS)** — High-Speed Message Services
- **SEMI E87/E90/E94** — Carrier/Substrate/Control job management
- **SEMI E5 (SECS-II)** — Equipment communication
- **SEMI F47** — Voltage sag immunity

## AIAG

- **MSA Reference Manual** — Gauge R&R, measurement system analysis
- **FMEA Reference Manual** — chuẩn FMEA (cùng VDA)
